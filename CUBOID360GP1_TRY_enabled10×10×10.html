Exploring Volume Through a Box of Eid Gifts
Instructions & Tasks
This is a model of a box of balls with dates and nuts, commonly shared in the UAE during Islamic occasions such as Ramadan, Eid, and family gatherings, in the shape of a cuboid.
Think and Answer
Look at just one flat layer of balls. What does the number of balls tell you? Is it same as Area (L×B)?
Look at how the layers are stacked. What does the number of layers tell you? Is it same as Height?
Now, think about the whole box. What does the total number of balls tell you? Is it same as Volume?
Use hand gestures to rotate and span the cuboid while exploring these concepts. Use the input boxes to enter the length, breadth, and height of the required cuboid. Disable the Span buttons to prevent accidental changes to the dimensions.
