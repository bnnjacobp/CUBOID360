A model of a cuboidal bread box is given. Your school has decided to donate it to charity.
Find the volume of the bread box by counting the unit cubes of breads filled in it!
• What is the dimension (length × breadth) of the base layer?
• What does the number of unit cubes in each layer represent?
• What does the number of layers represent?
• What does the total number of unit cubes in all the layers together represent?
• What do you obtain when you divide the volume of the cuboid by the surface area of its base layer?
• What do you obtain when you divide the volume of the cuboid by its height?
Use hand gestures to rotate and span the cuboid while exploring these concepts
